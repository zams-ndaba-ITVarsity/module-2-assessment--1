LOST FOREST

Head out for an enjoyable hike on this 2.1-km loop trail. Generally considered a moderately challenging route, it takes an average of 41 min to complete. This is a popular trail for hiking and walking, but you can still enjoy some solitude during quieter times of day.

On the east of the lost forest is a picnic area. The beautiful mini lake will draw your attention to this route for a quite recreation time.

WHERE WILL YOU FIND IT?

The lostforest is to the north of the monkey trail.
